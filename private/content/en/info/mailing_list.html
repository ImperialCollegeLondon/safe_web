This email list is for researchers working at SAFE to communicate with each other about their work. The group is moderated by Dr. Robert Ewers (email). All posts are archived and can be searched from here.

  1. To sign up, send a blank email (no subject and no content) to safeproject-general+subscribe@googlegroups.com
  2. To post to this group, send an email to safeproject-general@googlegroups.com
  3. To unsubscribe from this group, send an email to safeproject-general+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com
