It is a requirement of working at SAFE that you provide us with a copy of your data. We will validate your dataset and then publish it on Zenodo, which will :

  • provide a permanent home for your data,
  • assign it a permanent DOI, and
  • establish a permanent citation for your dataset.

Submission process

  1. Prepare your data for submission, following the instructions found here: https://safedata-validator.readthedocs.io.
  2. Note in particular:
    • Authorship on published datasets should be treated in the same way as you would consider authorship on papers: you should include not only the people responsible for physically collecting the data but also other researchers who facilitated the work, such as project supervisors and local collaborators.
    • Similarly, you must acknowledge any funding that supported your research.
    • The information you provide will become part of the permanent record on the published dataset - you should make the data and description as clear and informative as possible.
    • The datasets may be embargoed for up to two years, but metadata describing your dataset will be visible in Zenodo as soon as it is published.
  3. The file format will be checked automatically and you will be emailed with the result of the check.
  4. If the file has failed format checking, then follow the link in the email to see the check report. Once you've fixed any problems, come back and upload a new version. If a reported problem is unclear or doesn't seem like it should be a problem then email us to get clarification.
  5. If the file has passed format checking, then an administrator will look at the data before publishing it to Zenodo.

We realise it takes time and effort to get the data formatted correctly but the information you add is vital to make sure that we can archive your data properly and make it easy for future researchers to find it and benefit from it.
